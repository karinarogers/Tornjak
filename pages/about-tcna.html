About Tornjak Club

of North America

A small group of passionate Tornjak owners gathered to share their love of the breed in September 2023. This small meeting in Ontario, Canada became the very beginning of the Tornjak Club of North America.

TCNA is a community of Tornjak enthusiasts who have come together to share stories and knowledge about a rare and wonderful breed. Whether you're a seasoned Tornjak owner or just curious about these special pups, we're here to welcome you.

Our Club Standards

OUR MISSION

STATEMENT

It is the mission of the Tornjak Club of North America to promote and preserve the health and heritage of the Tornjak dog breed. Through education, awareness, and responsible breeding practices, we work to ensure the Tornjak population stays healthy and continues to grow within North America.

WE STAND FOR

ETHICAL BREEDING

Tornjaks were recently brought back from the brink of extinction. Their small population means unique breeding challenges. It is the responsibility of every Tornjak owner to properly health test and responsibly select breeding pairs to protect the health of this rare, amazing breed.

WE PROVIDE

INFORMATION

We strive to provide a supportive community, resources, and advocacy for Tornjak owners, breeders and admirers. Through education, awareness, and responsible breeding practices, we aim to foster a sense of belonging and purpose in the preservation of these extraordinary canine treasures.

Founded in

September 2023

May and September 2023 saw the first in-person meet-ups of Tornjak lovers in North America. May's meeting saw nine people and six tornjaks. September's meeting saw eleven people and eight tornjaks. It was at that second meeting where the Tornjak Club of North America was founded.
